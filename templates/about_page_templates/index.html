About

Triviafy your workspace.

Triviafy is a weekly trivia quiz sent to your team. Triviafy quizzes are guaranteed to spark new and interesting conversations among team members.

Triviafy has helped team members discover each others passions, interests, hobbies, talents, and hidden knowledge. Triviafy is a tool that will standardize and automate weekly trivia quizzes at the company-team level.

Benefits of weekly Triviafy quizzes: build team camaraderie, test and enhance your memory, increase brain performance, reduce stress levels, increase employee satisfaction at the company, improve company PR, earn bragging-rights, win free gift card prizes.

Triviafy writes well thought out questions across a broad range of categories, handles automated grading, and distributes rewards so that you do not have to. Triviafy saves you time, improves company culture, and strengthens your team.
